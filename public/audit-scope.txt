HumangoBot operates as a stateless security crawler designed to provide high-fidelity auditing for modern web infrastructure. Our discovery logic and content analysis are split into two primary reporting modules:

I. SaaS-COMPLIANCE MODULE (Automated fixes available)
------------------------------------------------------------
1. DOCUMENT DISCOVERY
   - Full site traversal beyond common paths.
   - Multilingual keyword analysis (privacy, cookies, terms, legal, impressum, conditions, ecc.)
   - Status: Marks "Violation: Document missing" if mandatory EU legal files are not found.

2. CONTENT SEMANTICS (GDPR ART. 13/14)
   - Mandatory Clauses: Verification of Data Categories, Processing Purposes, Retention Periods, Controller Contacts, and User Rights (Art. 15-22).
   - Recency Audit: Detection of document update dates. Documents older than 12 months are flagged as "Outdated".
   - Regulatory Mentions: Verification of local law citations (GDPR, UK GDPR, LOPDGDD, TMG).

3. LINGUISTIC TRANSPARENCY
   - Site Language vs. Document Language matching.
   - Flags "Violation: No local language" if policies do not match the interface language (e.g., FR site with EN policy).

II. TECHNICAL/MANUAL AUDIT MODULE (Direct implementation required)
------------------------------------------------------------
1. INFRASTRUCTURE SECURITY
   - SSL/TLS: Verification of HTTPS encryption on all pages.
   - Response Integrity: Monitoring 200 OK availability for all legal resources.
   - Header Audit: HSTS, CSP, and X-Frame-Options verification.

2. LINK INTEGRITY
   - Recursive scan of links inside legal documents to identify 404 (Broken Link) errors.

3. DYNAMIC PRIVACY (Deep Scan Mode)
   - Detection of tracking cookies and pixels set before user consent.
   - Analysis of CMP (Consent Management Platform) existence and behavior.

4. ACCESSIBILITY & IMAGES (Manual Report)
   - Alt-Tag Violations: Flagged in the "Direct/Manual" report as they require per-image correction.
   - Broken Media: Identification of 404 image resources.

III. BOT POLICIES (RFC 9309)
------------------------------------------------------------
- Identity: HumangoBot/1.0 (+https://humango.app)
- Politeness: Strict robots.txt adherence, 5s default delay, exponential backoff.
- Privacy: Sends DNT: 1 and GPC: 1 headers with every request.
- Stateless: No session storage, no cookies retained, no fingerprinting.

Contact for Opt-Out: abuse@humango.app
